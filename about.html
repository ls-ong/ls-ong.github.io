About Lian

Hiya!


I'm a designer in my second year at UC Berkeley, studying Computer & Cognitive Science with a particular interest in Design Innovation. I am fascinated by the ever blurring lines between technology and human cognition, and how technology can be used to augment and embody the intractable mind.

I strive to create emotive and accessible design solutions — to that end, I work as a product designer and serve as Director of Marketing for Berkeley Innovation, a student-run design consultancy here at Berkeley.

When I'm not 20 layers deep in my latest Sketch project, I'm out shooting photographs, sharpening my web design skills, and seeking new cafés to learn and create in. I also adore Sufjan Stevens, good coffee, birds and octopus, and anything written by Murakami.

Currently, I am working at Rainforest QA as a Product Design intern! Feel free to check out my resume and LinkedIn, and don't hesitate to shoot me a message at hello@liansong.co!
